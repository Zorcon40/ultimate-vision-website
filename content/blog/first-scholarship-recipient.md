We are thrilled to announce our first scholarship recipient through the Arby's P.A.W.S. (Purpose, Awareness, Willingness, Service) Scholarship program. This program recognizes students who demonstrate exceptional kindness and creativity in their communities.

## About the P.A.W.S. Scholarship

The Arby's P.A.W.S. Scholarship was established to honor students who embody the values that Ultimate Vision stands for: Purpose, Awareness, Willingness, and Service. We believe that by recognizing and supporting young people who demonstrate these qualities, we can encourage even more acts of compassion and kindness in our communities.

## Our First Recipient

Our first scholarship recipient has shown remarkable dedication to helping others and creating positive change in their school and community. Through various volunteer initiatives and creative projects, they have inspired their peers to embrace kindness and inclusion.

## How to Apply

Are you or someone you know making a difference through kindness and creativity? We encourage you to apply for the Arby's P.A.W.S. Scholarship! Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis, and recipients are announced quarterly.

Visit our scholarship page to learn more about eligibility requirements and the application process.

## Thank You for Your Support

Programs like the P.A.W.S. Scholarship are made possible through the generous support of our donors and community partners. Thank you for helping us inspire the next generation of compassionate leaders!
